RULES
COMPONENTS:
  1. Daily Quests
  2. Side Quests
  3. Special Quests
  4. Rewards
  5. Penalty
  6. Skill Points
  7. Level Up
LEVEL UP:
The Player levels up by earning XP through Quests. Player starts at level 1 with 0XP and requires 100XP to level up. The requirement increases by 1.5x till level 6 and changes to 1.2x afterwards.
XP:
XP for each quest can be viewed in "Current Status" page. XP remains same for all levels and can only be increased through Skill Points after level 9.
SKILL POINTS:
Player will earn 1 skill point for having a flawless streak of 12 days.

Skill point rewards:
  1. Reward Upgrade- Upgrade all rewards per quest by 1.1x
  2. XP Boost- Increase XP per quest by 1.3x
SKILLS to unlock:
  1. 500Rs per A.S- Level 7
  2. XP Boost- Level 9
  3. Per A.S 100 XP- Level 11
REWARDS:
Player earns rewards for Quest completed. Rewards can be viewed in the "Current Status". Rewards increments by 1.2x for every Level Up.
PENALTY:
  1. For every Daily Quest failed, the player must eat 1 spice without food and water for 20 minutes.
  2. Rewards for each Daily Quest will be deducted from the total balance. The balance can't go lower than 0.
  3. Every 4 Daily Quests failed in any 4 consecutive days will result in XP reset for that level.
Daily Quests:
  1. Workout:
    The player must complete a Workout session before 12 p.m. In case of failure, penalty will be given.
  2. Invitation:
    • Callings must be started before 2:30 p.m. The player must make a successful call with a lead to fulfill the condition. Failing will lead to Penalty.
    • A minimum of 20 calls must be completed by 4:30 p.m. Failing it will lead to Penalty. The player will have to eat half a spice for each condition above at 4:30 p.m.
    • To complete the Invitation Quest, 25 collective calls are to be completed before 8:30 p.m. Failing will lead to Penalty of eating a full spice.
    • Front Follow-Up will be counted as a completion of Daily Quest for a maximum of 5 days.
  3. Meditation:
    Player must meditate for at least 10-30 minutes by 12 a.m. Failing will lead to Penalty.
Note- For every Penalty Quest completed, the player will be awarded 10XP if every penalty is completed for that Quest on the day.
SPECIAL QUESTS:
The player is obligated to take up 2-3 Goals for the Month with deadlines.
Rewards- Average earning and XP in the last level up x2
Penalty- 1 Spice, no food and water for 30 minutes. The Balance will be reset to 0. Must be completed in the next 12 hours or by 12 p.m the next day.
EXTRA:
- The player is allowed to take 2 Leaves per Month
